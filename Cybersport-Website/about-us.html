PROGAMES

GAMING LEAGUE

GAMING IS ABOUT CONNECTING PEOPLE

PROGAMES has a major promise to the games industry and the CS:GO community it serves. We want to create deep, long-lasting connections with our users and advertise by producing quality results that operate with the highest possible outcome and commercial integrity.

In PROGAMES, we focus on users experience, team-work and skills first. PROGAMES core values are equality, respect, accuracy, fairness, and transparency. To ensure we maintain those standards, live by a strict code of ethics, that we follow:

We report stories as we see them, as accurately and honestly as possible

We always give a right to reply, correct any true errors led to our attention, and honor our sources clearly where appropriate.

We believe in being fair, honest and transparent in our dealings with users.

Third parties regularly verify the traffic on all PROGAMES sites, and we publish that information promptly.
If we visit any sort of "VIP" gaming event at any place outside of our rooms, we will report on it with full transparency. We believe the experience and conditions of the events to be as editorially relevant as the tournaments being covered.

CS:GO game and other items (merchandise) received through work may not be sold or traded. If they are no longer useful for work then they will be donated to local charities. Team and users are not allowed to do "mock reviews" for or provide consultancy to any publishers.

Staff and contributors may not write about a company they have worked for in any capacity within the last two years. If you think any distinct feature of how we work falls short of the above, or have issues with anything, please feel free to contact us here!
